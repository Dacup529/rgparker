About Me!

My name is Reese. I love design of all sorts. I especially love game and character design. I often spend my free time at home sketching up new ideas, spending time with my fiance, playing with our pets, and immersing myself in video games.

Chemical Z

Chemical Z was a scifi poster designed for my Graphic Design class at Wake Tech. We were to design a plot and poster for a scifi movie. The plot surrounded a group of space explorers who have gone to space to look for the previous exploration team only to discover they had been turned to zombies. They must now survive the onslaught of zombies and make it out alive.

Rising Sun Lager

Rising Sun Lager was made to be a label on a bottle or can. In this project I researched Japanese alcohol brands and types. Since pale lager beer was among the top of the list of favorites, I made my label revolve around lager with japanese themes.

Amityville Horror

Amityville Horror was a project designed from inkblots. I was to create a piece of art by casting inkblots on to a paper. I then needed to take those inkblots and find a book that reminded me of them. After I found the book, I was to create a book cover with the inkblots, title, and author of the book.

Mountainside

When I am not doing school projects, I am creating artwork to sell on Redbubble. Mountainside was one of my first pieces created with photography. I took a picture around the foothills of the Appalachian Mountains in North Carolina and I liked it so much that I turned it into a piece I could sell. Mountainside is among one of my most liked pieces on Redbubble.
